FROM THE SENIOR PASTOR

This great assignment was given birth to by the act of the Almighty God and was not orchestrated by any event of life or by human arrangement or agenda. The call for this assignment came to me in 2005 in a vision of the night when God reminded me of what He told me on the 5th of August, 2002, when I laid to pray at the Redemption Camp of the Redeemed Christian Church of God, that He has saddled me for a higher assignment, which shall unravel as time goes on. On the 5th of February 2006, in a vision of the night, the Lord instructed me that He had prepared Bishop Oyedepo of the Living Faith to speak to my life for the new phase of His assignment. Little did I know that God had spoken with His general. On getting to Canaan Land on a Sunday with the busy schedule of the man of God, he came out to pray for me and my wife (Pst (Mrs) Christy Ayo-Oluwagbemi). As soon as the Bishop came out, his first words were, "are you now ready for the assignment?" which was a surprise to both my wife and me. However, I did not still leave the Redeemed Christian Church of God because of my love for the church and because I needed to be very sure of the assignment.

  • The name of the ministry was given to me on 8th June 2008 as JOY Alive Christian Assembly .

I finally left the RCCG in October 2012 to face the assignment. In January 2013, I left for Akure, the capital city of Ondo State that is in the Western part of Nigeria, where the ministry started to do a spiritual mapping of the land and get a place for worship for the fresh work.
The fresh work finally began on the 15th of July 2013 with a 7-day crusade and the first Sunday service on the 21st of July, 2013 at Bolajoko Street, off Idanre Road, Oke Aro, Akure, Ondo State.
The ministry was officially commissioned on the 24th of August, 2014, by the Holy Spirit through His anointed servant Pastor Nelson Bushel, the President and Founder of Life Gate Ministry Int'l, Abeokuta, Ogun State.
The owner of this work (The Almighty God) extended the work to Asaba, Delta State, where a branch has been established. To the glory of God, the work has entered into an apostolic assignment into cities and villages with the name given JOY IN THE CITY CRUSADE.
On the 5th of March 2022, I was consecrated an Apostle by the Prelate, of Southern Nigeria Ecclesiastical Jurisdiction, COGIC, Bishop Henry C Praise. GLORY TO GOD.
Apostle Ayo Oluwagbemi
Senior Pastor
